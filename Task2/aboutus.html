UNESCO Heritage Sites in India

About Us

Welcome to our page dedicated to UNESCO Heritage Sites in India! We are passionate about preserving and promoting India's rich cultural and natural heritage. Our team is committed to providing accurate and up-to-date information about these sites, which are recognized by the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) for their outstanding universal value.

India is home to 42 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, with 34 cultural, 7 natural, and 1 mixed site. We aim to showcase the unique beauty and importance of each site, and encourage responsible tourism that benefits local communities and helps preserve these precious places for future generations.
